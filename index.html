Deep corridor
深廊

He will move on, and I will move on too.
We may go the same path, or maybe we will not.
We may never walk together, but there will be covert echo of our footsteps intertwined forward and backward in time.

他会继续前行，我也会继续前行。
我们可能走在同一条路上，也可能不会。
我们也许永远不会一起走，但我们的脚步声将在时光中交错，
指引与回溯，悄然相连。

Love is to gently kill

猫为了捕食杀死了一只鸟，我为了埋葬杀死了一朵花。
我爱猫，爱鸟，也爱花。
这就是我的爱。

Inner World 
内心世界



Submerge,

   沉没，

Sink,

   沉落，

Into the abyssal of deep lake.

沉入深湖的深渊，

Noise turned from white to black.

白噪音转换成黑噪音。

Lick a moment of peace by tasting suffocation,

通过品尝窒息来舔舐片刻的平静，

Smooth the pulsation of drumming in blood vessels,

平息血液中的锣鼓喧天，

Swallow the turmoil through throat into stomach.

将喉头的动荡吞咽入腹。

Give everything up,

放弃一切，

Let anything be,

让一切存在，

No longer exert and exhaust oneself towards the surface.

不再为了游向水面挣扎至筋疲力尽。


Listen carefully,

仔细聆听，

What emerges out of the deathly stillness.

有什么涌现出死寂。

Bubbles,

气泡，

Brushing past in the opposite direction,

从反方向擦身而过，

Combust methane into flames.

将甲烷燃烧成火焰。

Something has been erased and destroyed,

某些东西被抹去毁灭，

Irretrievably reduced to ashes.

无可挽回地化为灰烬。

As if words on the paper,

就像纸上的字迹，

Memory,

记忆，

Past,

过去，

Self,

自我，

What has made up the present me?

是什么组成了现在的我？

What has been left in me?

我还剩下些什么？


Walk along the deep corridor,

走过深深的长廊，

Endless road accompanied with darkness and silence.

黑暗与寂静常伴无尽的道路。

Step by step,

一步又一步，

Which step is doomed for collapse?

哪一步终将注定崩颓？

Locked in the panic room,

被反锁在恐慌室内，

Horror film played everywhere without exits.

到处都在播放着恐怖电影，没有出口。

Am I waiting for the moment breaking away from the nightmare,

我在等待从噩梦中挣脱出来的那一刻，

Praying for a dream that can at least wake up?

祈求这至少是一个能够醒来的梦境。

Disappear while elapsing,

流逝着消失，

Anguish until dissolving,

痛苦直至解体，

Leaving merely untraceable trace.

只留下不可追溯的踪迹。

Is my whole life no more than a hopeless and desperate wait,

我的整个人生只是一场无望又绝望的等待，

I am so weary of the turbulent and desolate trip.

我已厌倦了这场动荡而荒凉的旅行。

In the unknown ahead,

在未知的前方，

How many alters are there continuing to be torn and pieced?

还有多少个人格等待着被撕裂和重组？ 


Could anyone survive without subjectivity?

有谁能够不作为主体存在？

Freedom inward and freedom outward,

向内的自由和向外的自由，

Which level of the agency is being chased at the expense of others silenced?

哪一层自主性的实现不是以扼杀其他为代价？

Torture oneself in order not to be tortured by the external,

折磨自己以免被外界折磨，

Become the dictatorial in order to fight against the dictatorial.

成为独裁者以对抗独裁。

Nietzsche's dragon slayer and abyss.

尼采的屠龙者与深渊。

There is a fine line between establishment and destruction,

建设与毁灭仅一线之隔，

One can stand shoulder to shoulder with the gods while roaming on the misty sea,

一个人可以漫游雾海之上与众神比肩，

Yet can also get trapped in the hell built for oneself.

也可以深陷为自己建造的地狱。

A tree of life growing towards the two poles.

生命之树向两极生长。

Mankind is a creature longing for proclaiming one’s own truth,

人类是一种渴望宣扬自身真理的生物，

Yet without the other none of these truth ever exists,

然而真理从来不独立于他者存在，

Neither the one who advocates.

没有他者，真理的主张者亦不复存在。

There is a two-way channel between self and the world,

自我与世界之间是一条双向的通道，

A web of nexus that can never be undone.

一张永远无法被解除的关系之网。

When I drop from the insurmountable cracks,

当我从无法逾越的缝隙中跌落，

Is there someone who has ever fallen waiting to catch me in the deep?

是否有也曾坠落的人等在下面接住我？
